Community Bulletin

The town council is organizing multiple events to foster community spirit and participation. The upcoming events include an art exhibition at the public library, a community walk for health awareness, and weekend farmer’s markets to promote local produce.

Residents are encouraged to volunteer for various activities including clean-up drives, donation programs, and senior care outreach. These events aim to bring all age groups together and support the city’s goal of inclusive development.
